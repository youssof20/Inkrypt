## F-Droid

**Summary (short description, ~80 chars):**

**Short description (for store listing):**  
Inkrypt is a local-only, encrypted journal for Android. Your entries are stored on device and protected with a PIN. No account, no cloud, no analytics. Supports templates, search, tags, images, export to Markdown or encrypted ZIP, and a quick-capture widget.

**Full description (long, for F-Droid or Play):**  
Inkrypt keeps your journal private and on your device.

- **Encryption:** Content and database are encrypted (AES-GCM, SQLCipher). Key is derived from your PIN (PBKDF2).
- **Local only:** No servers, no account, no internet required. No analytics or crash reporting.
- **Features:** Create and edit entries, optional tags and images, reusable templates, local search. Export to Markdown or password-protected ZIP; import from the same formats. Optional biometric unlock. Quick-capture widget.
- **Open source:** MIT license. You can build from source and verify what the app does.

Requires Android 8.0+. No Google Play Services needed. Suitable for F-Droid.
